# Attimi Brisbane

**Date:** May 07, 2025 11:45 pm
**Instagram:** @attimi.brisbane

## Caption

@attimi.brisbane by @dariomancachef is a refined, intimate gem in Paddington that delivers an exceptional Italian degustation experience. Every dish is thoughtfully crafted, elegant on the plate and rich in flavour. 

My picks: the two unexpected stars of the night were the charcoal trout, understated in appearance but layered and delicate in taste, and the house-made butter infused with mussel. Yes, mussel. It was phenomenal - briny, buttery, and utterly addictive. I’d buy a jar if I could, no questions asked.

I also appreciated being the chief taster for a few experimental cocktails, an unexpected treat that made the experience feel even more personal and fun.

Pictured 📸:
1. Charcoal QLD coral trout, cardamom and prosciutto di Parma sauce, grapefruit, chilli oil, fennel fronds, puffed fennel barley
2. Caviar bomb scampi crudo, potato cream, chives, yolk, carasau chips
3. Housemade sourdough with house-infused butter
4. Beef rump cap tartare, black bagna cauda mayo, stracciatella, chilli oil, flatbread chard, pickled cocktail onions
5. Crispy potato layers, duck fat, smoked crème fraîche, chilli oil, chives
6. Carbonara burrata, burrata opera, carbonara foam, Pecorino Romano cream, crispy guanciale, black pepper, chive oil
7. Game farm rabbit cavatelli, homemade semolina pasta, rabbit ragu, chestnut butter, goat cheese cream, caper leaves, coffee jus
8. Attimi’s Caesar salad, caesar style, yolk, Pecorino Romano, Caesar dressing
