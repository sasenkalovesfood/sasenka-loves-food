# Penelope Bistro

**Date:** May 02, 2025 1:30 am
**Instagram:** @penelope.bistro

## Caption

@penelope.bistro is a chic addition to Brisbane's James Street, where classic bistro comfort meets late-night charm, right in the heart of James Street. Stylish without trying too hard, it’s the kind of spot that works for a long lunch, a moody dinner, or cocktails that turn into midnight snacks.

Pictured 📸:
1. Pastry, with goat cheese and chili
2. Scallop, Potato Scallop, Oscietra Caviar, Chives
3. Duck Paté, Pickled Sour Cherry, Toast
4. Chicken Tenders, Buttermilk Ranch, Hot Honey with Caviar
5. Steak Frites, Au Poivre, Cognac & Green Peppercorn Sauce
6. Pasta Alla Vodka, Smoked Mozzarella, ‘Nduja, Stracciatella
7. Sazerac with Rye and Tequila
