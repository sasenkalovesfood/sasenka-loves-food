## Caption

Finally got the opportunity to dine at @thefiftysixrestaurant by Chef @project_enoki at @naldhamhouse.

The Fifty Six brings a fresh take on Cantonese dining to Brisbane, blending tradition with local ingredients in a stunning riverside setting. Thoughtful dishes, great service, and a space that nods to the city’s Chinese heritage. Well worth the visit.

Thank you François for the amazing service as always 🙏🏽.

Pictured 📸:
1. Quail tea egg & avruga caviar with Oscietra caviar (must have!)
2. Moo shu duck pancakes with cucumber & house hoi sin (amazing!)
3. Raw Hiramasa kingfish, ginger, green chili & finger lime
4. Scallop & prawn siu mai
5. Steamed Murray cod, fragrant soy, ginger & scallions (so good!)
6. Sticky cumin Margra lamb riblets & eggplant
7. White chocolate, matcha & mandarine
8. Deep-fried toffee ice-cream with char siu caramel
