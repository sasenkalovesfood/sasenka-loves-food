# Stilts Dining

**Date:** Apr 20, 2025 6:03 pm
**Instagram:** @stilts.dining

## Caption

Trying out @stilts.dining, another restaurant by @michael_tassis. 

Stilts Dining in Brisbane offers an elevated dining experience, literally and figuratively. From beautifully plated dishes to a relaxed, elegant atmosphere, it’s the kind of spot where you slow down, savour every bite, and let the city fade away.

Pictured:
1. Confit beetroot with macadamia cheese and crackers
2. Pan seared Canadian scallops with morcilla, corn purée and crispy leeks
3. Stilts brioche with cultured butter and smoked salt
4. Toothfish with artichoke purée, artichoke crisps, fish broth and Australian native flowers
5. Moreton Bay bugs and linguine pasta
6. Cucumber salad with goat feta
7. Cheese because why not
