# HimalayanCafe

**Date:** May 05, 2025 11:45 pm

## Caption

The Himalayan Café in New Farm is the ultimate hidden gem for comfort food. This small, unassuming spot delivers big on flavour, with dishes that take your tastebuds to Himalayan heights - seriously, the momo alone is worth the climb. The service is warm and welcoming, and the prices are almost too good to be true. A cosy, no-fuss experience that hits the spot every single time.

Pictured 📸:
1. Momo Cha
2. Bakhara Himalayan
3. Himalayan Chili Bread
4. Phali
